Lots of Creative Commons events over the next week. Hope to see you at some of them.

Also, [Freesouls](http://freesouls.cc/) will go on sale at the Berkman event.

> Creative Commons Blog
>
> On Dec. 12th, 2008, CC will be pairing up with two of the most influential and innovative institutions in the "open" movement: [MIT's Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (CSAIL)](http://www.csail.mit.edu/) and [Harvard's Berkman Center for Internet & Society](http://cyber.law.harvard.edu/).
>
> For those of you interested in the tech side of Creative Commons, MIT's CSAIL is hosting [CC's second Tech Summit](http://wiki.creativecommons.org/Creative_Commons_Technology_Summit_2008-12-12) from 9-4:30. The first Tech Summit, held at Google this past summer, was a complete success; those archived presentations are [here](http://wiki.creativecommons.org/Creative_Commons_Technology_Summit_2008-06-18).
>
> And for those of you interested in CC generally, CC and Harvard's Berkman Center for Internet & Society have joined forces to bring you the panel discussion: "The Commons: Celebrating accomplishments, discerning futures." Panelists include [James Boyle](http://www.law.duke.edu/boylesite/), *[The Public Domain](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0300137400?ie=UTF8&tag=centeforthest-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=9325&creativeASIN=0300137400)*; [Lawrence Lessig](http://www.lessig.org), *[Remix](http://remix.lessig.org/)*; [Joi Ito](http://joi.ito.com/), *[Free Souls](http://freesouls.cc/)*; and [Molly S. Van Houweling](http://www.law.berkeley.edu/php-programs/faculty/facultyProfile.php?facID=5073), Creative Commons' first Executive Director. [Jonathan Zittrain](http://cyber.law.harvard.edu/people/jzittrain), of the Berkman Center for Internet & Society, will moderate. A reception will follow at 7:30 pm. Details are [here](http://wiki.creativecommons.org/Creative_Commons-Berkman_Panel_Discussion_and_Reception).
>
> We hope you will join us in celebrating Creative Commons' sixth successful year and the culmination of our 2008 Annual Fundraising Campaign, "[Build the Commons](http://support.creativecommons.org)." This event is open to the public, but because we're closing in on the end of our campaign, we encourage you to bring your check books (or cash rather) and help sustain CC by donating at the door!

> [CC is Turning 6! SF Birthday Party Announced!](http://creativecommons.org/weblog/entry/11037)
>
> To celebrate six exceptional years for CC and the December 31st wrap-up of our [2008 fundraising campaign](http://support.creativecommons.org), CC headquarters is hosting a birthday bash in San Francisco! The San Francisco party joins the ranks of [other global birthday parties](http://wiki.creativecommons.org/Birthday_Party_2008) in Berlin, Brisbane, Guatemala, Seoul, and New York. The San Francisco event will be held on Thursday, December 18th, 2008, from 9pm - 2am at [111 Minna Gallery](http://111minnagallery.com)([Map and Directions](http://maps.google.com/maps?hl=en&client=firefox-a&q=111+minna+gallery&ie=UTF8&z=12&iwloc=A)).

[NHK](http://www.nhk.co.jp/), the Japanese public broadcaster and the largest broadcaster in Asia, will use Twitter for a live TV special called "[SAVE THE FUTURE](http://www.nhk.or.jp/savethefuture/)". Twitter will be used to connect the studio and the viewing public.

The Twitter account is: [NHK\_onair](http://twitter.com/NHK_onair)

Also, [NHK recently started a YouTube channel](http://www.youtube.com/NHK). Go NHK! ;-)

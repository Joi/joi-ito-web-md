> [Times to End Charges on Web Site](http://www.nytimes.com/2007/09/18/business/media/18times.html?_r=1&ex=1347768000&oref=slogin)

The New York Times will stop charging for access to parts of its Web site, effective at midnight Tuesday night, reflecting a growing view in the industry that subscription fees cannot outweigh the potential ad revenue from increased traffic on a free site./cheer

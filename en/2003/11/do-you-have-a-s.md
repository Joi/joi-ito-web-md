Another great line from ["the effects of mobile telephones on social and individual life"](http://www.motorola.com/mot/doc/0/234_MotDoc.pdf) by Dr. Sadie Plant.
> Dr. Sadie Plant
>
> several people confessed to using two mobile handsets, one for general use and the other for affairs; one respondent in London referred to the latter as a **shagbile**.

[rvr](http://rvr.blogalia.com/) just modified [jibot](http://joi.ito.com/joiwiki/JiBot), the resident bot on the [#joiito irc channel](http://joi.ito.com/joiwiki/IrcChannel) to allow you to post to [#joiito bot blog](http://hashjoiito.bloxus.com/) from IRC. (It's using the [Blogger API](http://xmlrpc.free-conversant.com/docs/bloggerAPI)). #joiito bot blog is running on [Bloxus](http://www.bloxus.com/), a blog package developed by rvr.

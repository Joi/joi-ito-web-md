![](http://joi.ito.com/images/arnett.jpg)[The New York Times](http://www.nytimes.com/2003/03/31/international/worldspecial/31CND-ARNE.html), [The Washington Post](http://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/articles/A57936-2003Mar31.html) and [USA Today](http://www.usatoday.com/news/world/iraq/2003-03-31-arnett-fired_x.htm) report NBC News fires Arnett Over Iraqi TV Interview. Via [The Command Post](http://www.command-post.org/archives/002971.html), here is the [official word from National Geographic](http://news.nationalgeographic.com/news/2003/03/0331_030331_arnettfired.html) which co-fired him.

Update from [The Command Post](http://www.command-post.org/archives/002998.html): "Peter Arnett, the American reporter fired by MSNBC and National Geographic earlier today has reportedly (Fox News) been hired by the Daily Mirror."

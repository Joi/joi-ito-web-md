I just joined the [advisory board](http://www.mindjack.com/about/advisors.html) of [Mindjack](http://www.mindjack.com/), a cool online digital culture magazine. [Donald Melanson](http://www.melanson.ca/) is the Founder and Editor-in-Chief and Dan Richards is the Senior Editor. The other advisory board members are Gareth Branwyn, Mark Frauenfelder, Mikki Halpin, Jon Lebkowsky, Howard Rheingold, Douglas Rushkoff.

We received funding from the Japanese government to produce a global report on privacy technology and legislation. The report is called "A Report of Research on Privacy for Electronic Government." We tried to get the best experts around the world to help us on this report. Please take a look at it. It is available for download in its entirety under the [Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 1.0 license](http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-sa/1.0/).

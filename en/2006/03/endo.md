Adriaan, who works for me at Kula and is the author of the blog editing tool [Ecto](http://kula.jp/software/ecto/) and [1001](http://kula.jp/software/1001/), just released [Endo](http://kula.jp/software/endo/) his new aggregator/feed reader. Check it out when you have a chance.

Make sure you try smart groups and don't expect it to behave like a normal 3-pane application. ;-)

[More about it on his blog](http://blog.kung-foo.tv/archives/001660.html).

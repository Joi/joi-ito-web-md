The Stanford Center for Internet and Society filed an amicus brief today which I signed together with a number of others. Go CIS!
> [Amicus Brief Asks for Legal Rights for Internet Journalists](http://cyberlaw.stanford.edu/archives/2005_04.shtml#003034)

CIS filed an amicus brief today on behalf of The First Amendment Project, Internet journalists and bloggers and others asking the court in the [Apple v. Does](http://www.eff.org/Censorship/Apple_v_Does/) case to treat online publishers the same rights as their colleagues who publish in more traditional formats. [Download file](http://cyberlaw.stanford.edu/archives/Amicus%20Curiae.pdf)

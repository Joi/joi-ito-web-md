Hilary Rosen [[WP](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hilary_Rosen)], the former president and CEO of the [Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA)](http://www.riaa.com/default.asp) is guest blogging over at [Lawrence Lessig's blog](http://www.lessig.org/blog/archives/003086.shtml).

She follows Jimmy Wales, founder of Wikipedia, on the slate of excellent guest bloggers during Larry's summer vacation.

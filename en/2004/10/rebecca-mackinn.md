Rebecca MacKinnon is a the former bureau chief for CNN in Japan and now a fellow at the Harvard Berkman Center for Internet & Society. She's one of the people I turn to when trying to understand the future of journalism and she [writes about](http://rconversation.blogs.com/rconversation/2004/10/my_thougts_abou.html) some of the difficulties [Wikinews](http://meta.wikimedia.org/wiki/Wikinews) will have and provides some thoughtful suggestions.

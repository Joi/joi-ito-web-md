If you're using OS X, you can now search for Creative Commons content using Sherlock! Just connect to <sherlock://drop.creativecommons.org/sherlock/ccsearch.xml>

[![Ccsherlock](http://joi.ito.com/images/ccsherlock-tm.jpg "Ccsherlock")](http://joi.ito.com/images/ccsherlock.png)

If you don't have OS X, you can still use our [search engine](http://search.creativecommons.org/) to find licensed content.

More info on the [CC blog](http://creativecommons.org/weblog/entry/4471).

> Meriam-Webster declared yesterday that based on lookups in their online dictionary, the ["#1 Word of the Year for 2004"](http://m-w.com/info/04words.htm) is (drumroll and eyeroll)... "blog."  
> > Blog noun [short for Weblog] (1999) : a Web site that contains an online personal journal with reflections, comments, and often hyperlinks provided by the writer.
>
>   
> Also-rans include "incumbent,""insurgent,""hurricane," and "peloton," defined as the "main body of riders in a bicycle race."

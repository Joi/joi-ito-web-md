> In news at once frightening and reassuring, a Sequoia electronic voting machine suffered a very public [failure](http://www.wired.com/news/evote/0,2645,64569,00.html) last week during a live demo. The machine worked fine with an English-language ballot, but failed to record votes with the Spanish-language ballot.

I still think [electronic voting is a bad idea](http://joi.ito.com/archives/2003/09/20/ieee_members_save_democracy_from_a_broken_standardscommittee.html). Here is yet another example of a failure.

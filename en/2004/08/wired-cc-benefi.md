> Creative Commons Weblog
>
> [Wired presents a Creative Commons benefit in NYC, Sept 21st](http://creativecommons.org/weblog/archive/2004/08/#4370)
>
> Tuesday, September 21, 2004, Wired Magazine will throw a benefit for Creative Commons featuring a concert by David Byrne (with the Tosca Strings) and Gilberto Gil. It will take place at 8PM at [The Town Hall](http://www.the-townhall-nyc.org/) in New York City. Proceeds from the concert will go to support the non-profit efforts of Creative Commons.
>
> Tickets are available now from [Ticketmaster](http://www.ticketmaster.com/event/030038FCA6482288?brand=&artistid=734679&majorcatid=10001&minorcatid=60) or, after September 1st, at the [Town Hall](http://www.the-townhall-nyc.org/) box office. If you're in NYC and want to help support the work of the Creative Commons, come on out and enjoy a great concert.

Be there or be square.

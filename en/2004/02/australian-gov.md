Roger Clarke, one of my favorite privacy experts, [rips apart the Australian Government's attempt to make their face recognition technology trial look good](http://www.anu.edu.au/people/Roger.Clarke/DV/SmartGate040207.html). Face recognition systems have not been found to work well and are very intrusive. Here's another attempt to make them look better than they are.

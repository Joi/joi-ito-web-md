[Larry](http://www.lessig.org/) showed me [mozCC](http://www.yergler.net/projects/mozcc/), a cool plugin for Mozilla based browsers that shows the [Creative Commons](http://creativecommons.org/) license of a page on the browser window margin and also lets you examine the license in more detail. Can someone make this for IE and Safari?

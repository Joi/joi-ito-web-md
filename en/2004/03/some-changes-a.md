I have decided to discontinue additional investments from [Neoteny](http://blog.neoteny.com/neoteny/) and focus on supporting its current portfolio of companies. In particular, I believe that [Six Apart](http://www.sixapart.com/) has enormous potential and most of Neoteny's resources, including me will be focused on helping to make Six Apart a success.

[Six Apart](http://www.sixapart.com/) announced [TypeKey](http://www.typekey.com/). It's a user login system that can among other things, help prevent comment spam. It will work with [Movable Type 3.0](http://www.movabletype.org/news/2004_03.shtml#000912) and [TypePad](https://www.typepad.com/t/app/home).
